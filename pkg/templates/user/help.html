To create a savings group:
  • Go to the Groups section from your dashboard
  • Click the "Create Group" button
  • Fill in the group details (name, category, contribution amount, frequency)
  • Set the maximum number of members
  • Click "Create Group" to finalize

💡 Tip: Groups with 5-10 members are most effective.

To join a group:
  • Browse available groups in the Groups section
  • Click "Join Group" on the group you're interested in
  • Wait for the group admin to approve your request
  • Once approved, you'll be notified and can start contributing

💡 Some groups may require you to meet certain criteria before joining.

To make a contribution:
  • Go to your group's page
  • Click the "Make Contribution" button
  • Enter the amount you wish to contribute
  • Select your payment method
  • Confirm the transaction

💡 Contributions are typically due on the specified frequency (weekly, monthly, quarterly).

Yes, your money is safe with us. We implement:
  • Secure encryption for all transactions
  • Two-factor authentication for account protection
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • 24/7 fraud detection systems
  • Transparent transaction history available at all times

💡 We also recommend enabling 2FA for an extra layer of security.

If you can't make a contribution:
  • Contact your group admin immediately
  • Work out a temporary solution or payment plan
  • Consistently missed contributions may affect your standing in the group
  • If issues persist, you may be removed from the group

💡 We recommend setting up automatic payments to avoid missing contributions.
