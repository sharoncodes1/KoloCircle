Frequently Asked Questions

View all

Creating a savings plan is simple:

  • Go to the Savings section from the sidebar
  • Click the "New Plan" button
  • Choose your plan type: Flexible, Locked, or Target
  • Set your target amount and preferred duration
  • Start saving instantly!

Your savings will start earning interest from day one.

We charge a transparent management fee of 1.5% per annum on investment portfolios.

  • No hidden charges - What you see is what you get
  • No exit fees - Withdraw your funds anytime
  • No setup fees - Start investing with zero cost

All fees are clearly displayed before you confirm any investment.

Joining a savings group is easy:

  • Navigate to the Groups section
  • Browse available groups that match your interests
  • Click "Join Group" on any open group
  • Wait for admin approval (you'll receive a notification)
  • Start contributing and saving together!

You can also create your own group and invite others to join.

Yes! Your money is 100% safe. We use multiple layers of security:

  • 256-bit encryption for all data transmission
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Real-time fraud monitoring to detect suspicious activity
  • SEC licensed and regulated
  • Funds held in trust with licensed banks

Your funds are always separate from our operational accounts.

Withdrawing your savings is simple:

  • Go to your Savings dashboard
  • Select the savings plan you want to withdraw from
  • Click the "Withdraw" button
  • Enter the amount you wish to withdraw
  • Confirm the transaction

Funds are typically sent to your linked bank account within 24-48 hours.

You can start saving with as little as ₦1,000!

  • No minimum balance required to maintain your account
  • No monthly fees - Keep all your savings
  • Flexible contributions - Save as much or as little as you want

This makes it easy for everyone to start building wealth, regardless of their income level.
